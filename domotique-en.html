Using Node-Red to automate tasks
"Heating" Node-Red Flow

The "Heating" flow above allows:

  • To turn on the toilet heating during the week between 6:30 am and 10:00 pm, and on weekends between 8:00 am and 11:00 pm
  • To check that the window is not open when the heating is on
  • To apply temperature and heating curves based on variables defined in Home Assistant
"Presence" Node-Red Flow

The "Presence" flow above allows:

  • Check if someone is present and turn on the lights if necessary
  • Check if the room's light intensity is lower than the setpoint (adjustable in Home Assistant)
  • Manage light intensity and color temperature based on the time of day (adjustable in Home Assistant)
  • Possibility to lock the light (from Home Assistant) so it doesn't turn off anymore (ambient light)
